    ASHEVILLE – Venus Williams’ 7-5, 6-1 match win over the Netherlands’ Richel Hogenkamp wrapped up the U.S. Team’s first round Fed Cup win Sunday at the U.S. Cellular Center.

    It was Williams’ second match win of the weekend after defeating Arantxa Rus 6-1, 6-4 in the opening match Saturday. The win marked her 1,000-career match.

    "It's been an incredible weekend," U.S. team captain Kathy Rinaldi said. 

    CoCo Vandeweghe gave the USA a 2-0 lead in the second singles match Saturday, defeating Hogenkamp 4-6, 7-6(6), 6-3.

    With the USA already secured a first-round win, Vandeweghe’s singles match against Arantxa Rus will be skipped, making way for Serena Williams’ return to tennis in doubles after a year’s absence. It was announced after Venus' win that she will play with her sister, Serena, in the double match, ending the exciting weekend of tennis in Asheville on a high note.

    Venus Williams vs. Richel Hogenkamp 

    More: USA takes 2-0 lead in Day 1 of Fed Cup

    1st Set: Venus Williams wins 7-5

    - Williams takes a 1-0 lead on her serve, fighting back from a game-point from Hogenkamp.

    - Williams breaks Hogenkamp's serve, takes 2-0 early lead in Set 1

    - Williams goes up 3-0, does not lose a point in third game. 

    - Hogenkamp fights back on her serve and wins a game. She trails Williams 3-1.

    - Hogenkamp breaks Williams' serve without allowing Williams to score a point. Williams leads 3-2.

    - Hogenkamp wins her third straight game to tie things up at 3-3. Williams serving to take back the lead and grab back the momentum.

    - Williams serve hits 112 MPH as she takes back the lead, 4-3, over Hogenkamp. 

    - Hogenkamp ties the match at 4-4 in Set 1. Williams back to serve. 

    - Williams takes a 5-4 lead (serve now hitting 117 MPH), holding off Hogenkamp's rally after she took a 40-0 lead in the game. Williams can win Set 1 by breaking her opponent in the next game.

    - Wiliams fights back to force deuce, but Hogenkamp wins the next two points to tie the match at 5-5. Hogenkamp continues to be a thorn in the side of the Americans.

    - An incredible back-and-forth game, as Williams fights off game-point from Hogenkamp and comes back to take a 6-5 lead. Williams' serve, which was inconsistent in her win over Rus Saturday, has been her best weapon today.

    - Williams takes the set, 7-5, breaking Hogenkamp and not allowing her to score a point

    Set 2: Williams wins 6-1

    - Williams take momentum into second set, take 1-0 game lead on her serve. She's won 11 straight points.

    - Hogenkamp holds serve. Set 2 tied at 1-1

    - Williams' serve continues to be great. She leads 2-1.

    - Williams breaks Hogenkamp for the first time in Set 2 to take a 3-1 lead. Williams can give USA a first round Fed Cup win if she holds serve throughout the rest of the match.

    - Williams extends her lead to 4-1, two games away from a Fed Cup first round win for USA

    - Williams wins the final two games to win the match 7-5, 6-1
