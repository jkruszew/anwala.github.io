South Florence softball team hosts benefit golf tournament March 17

  • From staff reports
  • Feb 11, 2018
  • (…)
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Email
  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Email
  • Print
  • Save

South Florence’s softball team is hosting a benefit golf tournament March 17 at Crossings Golf Club. Costs are $200 per team.

The morning tournament starts at 8 a.m., and the afternoon tournament starts at 1 p.m.

Lunch will be served between the morning and afternoon rounds.

For more information, please call Bobby Jones at 843-992-0308 or Brooke Elliott at 843-269-4420.
