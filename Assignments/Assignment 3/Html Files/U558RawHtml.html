JCJC sets football tryouts for Feb. 15, Feb. 22

Feb 10, 2018

ELLISVILLE – Jones County Junior College will hold football tryouts on Feb. 15 and Feb. 22.

Registration will be from 9:30-10 a.m. at Bobcat Stadium/Sim Cooley Field. Cost is $10 per participant – cash or check only.

Participants must be a high school senior or a college student with remaining junior college eligibility. Players must bring their own cleats and a copy of their current physical.

For more information, contact the JCJC Football Office at 601-477-1158.

 

Shawn Wansley

Sports Information Director
