Published on Feb 12, 2018

The opening ceremony of the 2018 Winter Olympics kicked off on Friday, February 9 in PyeongChang, South Korea. The Nigerian team are the first African team to ever qualify for Olympics in this sport…The three representatives, Ngozi Onwumere, Akuoma Omeoga, Seun Adigun and Simi Adeagbo, who will represent the country in Bobsled and Skeleton respectively made a grand entry into the ceremony in their green white green dresses, black high boots which they paired with green gele.
