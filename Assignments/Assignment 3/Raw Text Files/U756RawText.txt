Date published: Monday 12th February 2018 1:59
Two Manchester United players top the list of football shirts sold by SportsDirect.com since January 1.
Alexis Sanchez has immediately claimed top spot after his January switch from Arsenal, with teammate Paul Pogba in second.
Liverpool pair Mo Salah and Roberto Firmino also feature in a top ten where they were joined by new Barcelona man Philippe Coutinho.
The most popular Arsenal shirt is that of Mesut Ozil, even after the signings of Henrikh Mkhitaryan and Pierre-Emerick Aubameyang.
The full list:
1) Alexis Sanchez – Manchester United
2) Paul Pogba – Manchester United
3) Harry Kane – Tottenham
