“Liverpool, Tottenham and Manchester City men are among the favourites to win this year's PFA Player of the Year award”
The prestigious award, chosen by members of the players’ trade union, is given to the star perceived as the best in English football over the past season.
N’Golo Kante scooped the prize last year after playing a crucial role in Chelsea’s 2017 Premier League title triumph.
Other past winners include Real Madrid superstar and former Manchester United man Cristiano Ronaldo, Arsenal great Thierry Henry and Liverpool hero Steven Gerrard.
But who will be named PFA Player of the Year for 2017/18?
